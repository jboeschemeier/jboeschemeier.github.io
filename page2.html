About This Webpage

This webpage was made for the ICT, Labour and Inequality course at the School of Business and Economics in Maastricht. Every post on this website centers around the topic of Information-Communication-Technologies, specifically automation and new innovations, as well as their impact on our society, labour market and equality.

About Me

Hey! I am Jonas, 20 years old, and currently in the second year of my Bachelor, studying Economics at Maastricht University. I am very fascinated by new technologies and innovations and believe that those will be the main cause for better living standards in the future. Find more about me here.
